About Me

        

Who am I?

My name is Subeka Chhabra, and I am thrilled to be a part of your academic journey. Teaching Physics is not just my profession; it is my true passion. With over nineteen years of dedicated experience in the field, I possess a wealth of knowledge that allows me to provide exceptional academic expertise to my students.

My teaching journey has encompassed tutoring both small and large groups, providing private individual tutoring, and teaching in both traditional classroom and home settings. This breadth of experience has enriched my understanding of various learning styles and enables me to tailor my approach to meet the unique needs of each student.



My Goal

With a deep-rooted enthusiasm for Physics, I strive to foster a genuine interest and appreciation for the subject among my students. I believe in creating an engaging and interactive learning environment, where students are encouraged to ask questions, explore concepts, and develop critical thinking skills.

Above all, my ultimate goal as a Physics educator is to empower my students to become independent learners, capable of applying their knowledge beyond the confines of the classroom. I take great pride in witnessing their growth and achievements, and I am dedicated to supporting their educational journey every step of the way.



        

My Education

I hold a Masters in Physics from Delhi University, India. Additionally, I have completed Bachelors in Education with a focus on Physics equipping me with comprehensive knowledge in the subject matter.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of teaching Physics to students from diverse backgrounds and across the globe including India, Australia and the US, in a multitude of settings.
